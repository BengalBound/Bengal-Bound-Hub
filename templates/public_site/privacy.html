Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026  ·  BengalBound Ltd

1. Who We Are

BengalBound Ltd ("BengalBound", "we", "us", "our") operates the BengalBound HUB platform — a multi-tenant SaaS Business Operating System with AI Agent services — accessible at bengalbound.com and associated subdomains. We are registered in Bangladesh and operate globally.

Data controller contact: privacy@bengalbound.com

2. What Data We Collect

Account data: Name, email address, password (hashed), company name, phone number — collected when you create an account.

Business data: Information you enter into business modules (CRM contacts, HR records, invoices, etc.) — stored on your behalf as a data processor.

AI interaction data: Conversations with AI Agents, AI-generated outputs, and related activity logs — used to deliver the service and improve safety.

Usage data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent — collected via server logs and analytics for security and product improvement.

Payment data: Billing address and payment method — processed directly by Stripe or SSLCommerz. We never store raw card numbers.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and operate the BengalBound platform
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • To process payments and send billing communications
  • To power AI Agent features on your behalf
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, alerts, password resets)
  • To improve the platform through aggregated, anonymised analytics
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your business data to train AI models without explicit consent.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR / UK GDPR)

  • Contract performance — processing necessary to deliver the service you signed up for
  • Legitimate interests — security monitoring, fraud prevention, product analytics
  • Legal obligation — tax records, compliance with court orders
  • Consent — marketing emails (you can withdraw at any time)

5. Data Retention

  • Active account data: retained for the duration of your subscription
  • After account deletion: anonymised within 30 days, fully purged within 90 days
  • Payment records: retained 7 years for tax/accounting obligations
  • AI Agent logs: retained 12 months, then deleted

6. Who We Share Data With

We use the following sub-processors:

  • Supabase — database hosting (EU/US servers)
  • Render — application hosting (US servers)
  • Netlify — static site hosting (US CDN)
  • Groq / LiteLLM — AI inference (prompts processed, not stored)
  • Stripe — payment processing (global)
  • Brevo — transactional email
  • Cloudflare — DNS, CDN, DDoS protection

All sub-processors are bound by GDPR-compliant Data Processing Agreements.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Restriction — limit how we process your data
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — for marketing emails, unsubscribe at any time

Submit requests to: privacy@bengalbound.com. We respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential: Session authentication, CSRF protection — cannot be disabled
  • Functional: Currency preference, UI settings
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (with your consent)

You can manage cookie preferences via the banner shown on first visit.

9. International Transfers

Data may be processed in the US (Render, Stripe, Groq) and the EU (Supabase). All transfers comply with GDPR Chapter V via Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

10. Security

  • All data in transit encrypted via TLS 1.2+
  • Sensitive fields (API keys, OAuth tokens) encrypted at rest using Fernet (AES-128)
  • Account lockout after 5 failed login attempts
  • Two-factor authentication (TOTP) available for all accounts
  • Immutable audit trail on all privileged operations

11. Children's Privacy

BengalBound is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us immediately.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will notify registered users by email at least 14 days before material changes take effect. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance.
