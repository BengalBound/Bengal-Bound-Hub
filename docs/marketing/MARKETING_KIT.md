## 🎯 Positioning Statement

*   **Target Audience:** SMEs, agencies, e-commerce stores, healthcare clinics, and social enterprises in Bangladesh and globally.
*   **The Problem:** Small businesses cannot afford full teams of specialists (SDRs, CS, Copywriters, HR) and struggle with constant staff turnover.
*   **Our Solution:** An instant-activation marketplace of 30 specialized AI employees that work 24/7/365, cost 10–50x less than human salaries, and are supervised by **Inspector** to guarantee 100% compliance with data privacy regulations (GDPR, HIPAA, and BD Cybersecurity Guidelines).
