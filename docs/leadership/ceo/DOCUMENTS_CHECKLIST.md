## SECTION C: Banking Documents Required

### BDT Current Account Requirements:
*   Trade License (2 copies + original)
*   Certificate of Incorporation (2 copies + original)
*   MoA + AoA (2 copies each)
*   Form XII (2 copies)
*   TIN Certificate (2 copies)
*   Board Resolution (authorising account opening)
*   Company Seal Impression

### Foreign Currency (FC / USD) Account Requirements:
*   All BDT Current Account documents
*   ERC Certificate (2 copies)
*   Explanation of export business on company letterhead
